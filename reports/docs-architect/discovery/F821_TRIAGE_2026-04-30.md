## Why these are "BUG" not "FALSE-POSITIVE"

- No `if TYPE_CHECKING:` block in this file. Imports are listed plainly at the top.
- No `from __future__ import annotations` at top of file → annotations are evaluated eagerly anyway, but the references in question are runtime expressions (`Badge(...)` calls, `BadgeCategory.ADVERTISER` enum access), not annotations.
- No dynamic imports / `globals()` / `setattr` / fixture-injection in scope.
- The names are simply not imported. Ruff is correct.

The only mitigation is the `@pytest.mark.skip` — the test never runs, so the bug never fires. That makes these **dormant bugs**, not false positives.
